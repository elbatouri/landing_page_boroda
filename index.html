Barber Booking Project

Streamlining the process of booking appointments with barbers

Features About Contact

Key Features

Feature 1 : booking

Simplify your barber appointment experience with our intuitive booking feature. Easily select your preferred date and time, choose a specific barber, and confirm your appointment hassle-free.

Feature 2 : bookings list

Stay organized with our 'View All Appointments' feature. Get a comprehensive overview of your upcoming and past appointments in one place. Filter and sort based on dates, barbers, or services for a convenient experience

Feature 3 : comment

Share your thoughts and experiences with our community! Leave comments and opinions about your recent barber visits. Your feedback matters, and it helps both barbers and other clients in making informed decisions

About Us

This project was inspired by the difficulties of booking appointments with my barber through phone calls. It became challenging for both me and my barber to manage all the bookings efficiently.
