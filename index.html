Barber Booking Project

Streamlining the process of booking appointments with barbers

Key Features

  • Booking with Barber: Easily schedule appointments with your preferred barber.
  • Sign In and Sign Up: Securely create an account or log in to manage your bookings.
  • View All Appointments: Access a comprehensive list of all your scheduled appointments.
  • Comments and Opinions: Share your thoughts, comments, and opinions about your experiences.

Get Started
